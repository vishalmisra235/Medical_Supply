Optimizing the medicine usage between shops

As we know medicine wastage is a huge problem in our scoiety. Every year large amounts of medicines have to be discarded due to their expiry. We aim to optimize the medicine supply between shops so that we can reduce wastage of those medicines whose expiry date is near by supplying it to the high demand shop.
